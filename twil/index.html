This week I learned

Excerpts from my development journal

In June of 2018, I started an experiment — writing a development journal — and this site is one of the byproducts. I have long been noticing how I learn neat tricks, but never entirely incorporate them into my workflow, so I end up forgetting them. To aid my memory, I started jotting these down in the journal. This site is a collection of such notes, grouped by week.
